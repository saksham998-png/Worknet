Quick start guide

  • 1. Complete onboarding — name your workspace and invite teammates.
  • 2. Create a project — from the dashboard, add tasks and assign owners.
  • 3. Use Kanban or calendar — drag cards to reprioritize; due dates show on the calendar.
  • 4. Collaborate — comment with @username to mention teammates; watch the activity feed.
  • 5. Report progress — charts and CSV/PDF exports for stakeholders.
